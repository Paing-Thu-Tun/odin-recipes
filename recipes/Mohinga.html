Mohinga is our national pride. It is a fish soup with fermented thin rice rice noodles. t all starts with making a smooth flavorful broth from extracting natural sweet juices of fleshy fish, ideally catfish, combined with other spices and aromatics.

Ingredients

  • Fish (catfish)
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Dried rice vermicelli - cooked per package instructions
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil fish
  2. Pound Garlic and Ginger together to make a paste
  3. Heat the pan, add oil and then add the paste
  4. Debone boiled fish and put in the pan
  5. Add water
